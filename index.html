About US

Clean air ducts and properly maintained dryer vents are essential for a healthy, safe, and energy-efficient home. At Atlantic Vents Cleaning , we specialize in professional air duct cleaning and dryer vent cleaning services that improve your indoor air quality and reduce fire hazards.

Our certified technicians use advanced equipment and proven techniques to remove dust, allergens, lint, and debris from your vent systems — ensuring your home stays comfortable and safe year-round.

  • Air duct cleaning for residential and commercial properties
  • Dryer vent cleaning and lint removal
  • Chimney cleaning and chimney sweep services
  • HVAC system cleaning and maintenance
  • Attic insulation and bird guard installation
  • Video duct inspection for thorough diagnostics

We're proud to be Staten Island's most trusted duct cleaning service, known for our affordable pricing, attention to detail, and commitment to customer satisfaction.
